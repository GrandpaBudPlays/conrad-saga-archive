**[Ulf's Voice]**

**[Conrad's Chronicle]**
The air in the Othala-Garth grew cold as I struck down a rattling shadow, its bone fragments heavy in my hands. I found The Golden Ones humming in the eaves and brought their queen to our Othala to provide The Queen’s Gift we need. This beast taming of the tusk-beasts with the strength of stars was a test of patience, their grunts softening as they tasted the sower's tithe of red berries. Deep within the earth, the Burial Chamber smelled of ancient dust, but the five glowing hearts I pulled from the dark will soon feed the forge to craft stone-marrow tools. I touched the cold stone of the Vegvisir and felt the weight of The Forsaken king pressing against my mind. The night in the forest was a blur of heavy footfalls and the stench of a shadow in the trees, my lungs heaving as I fled toward the safety of our hearth and home. Now, wrapped in elder wisdom and mended leather, I listen to the wind and prepare for the trial ahead.

**[Grandpa's Legend]**
Well, it was a busy day for our young exile, and I had to start by fixing up that workbench roof so the rain wouldn't keep us from our work. We managed to bring home a queen bee with a lucky bow shot and set up a few more beehives to keep the honey flowing. The real trick was leading those boars—one of 'em had the strength of stars—into the pen, where we're taming them with some fresh mushrooms and berries. I felt a bit of a hitch-in-my-gitty-up when that Troll chased us out of the Black Forest, but we made it back with five Surtling Cores and the location of The Elder. It’s a bitter lesson to learn that the woods get mean after dark, but with our new leather gear and a level three workbench, we're finally starting to feel at home.
