**[Grandpa's Legend]**
It is a quiet start to a long journey, yup. I found myself in the Meadows, far from the life I knew, with nothing but the clothes on my back and a bit of hope for the future. I met a talking bird—Hugin, he calls himself—and found a stone that points the way to a great storm-beast. I am no master of the stone-marrow yet, but I managed to fashion a decent axe and a place to lay my head. It is important to have a warm hearth when the night-terrors come knocking, especially when you are building a legacy for those you love back home.

**[Conrad's Chronicle]**

The journey begins with the discovery of Eikthyr’s location and the crafting of essential tools like the stone axe and hammer. Conrad secures his first resources from the local wildlife and establishes a humble homestead, complete with a workbench and a campfire. The day concludes as he sets his spawn point and finds a moment of peace in his new bed, earning the rested buff for the challenges to come.
