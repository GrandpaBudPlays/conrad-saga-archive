# 📝 Triple-Threat Description: S01 E001

## 🪓 The Narrative

**[Ulf's Voice]**
The Valkyrie drops the exile into the green waste. The stone axe bites the wood. The fire keeps the night at bay.

**[Grandpa's Legend]**
Yup, landing in the Meadows after that Valkyrie ride is enough to rattle any old soul. I started simple, crafting a Stone Axe (Founders' Relics) to clear the brush and finding some Flint along the water to sharpen my resolve. It wasn't long before I found the Vegvisir (The Guide-Stone) for Eikthyr (The Forsaken) and located his sacrificial altar. I made sure to build a proper Home Base (Othala Sanctuary) with a workbench and a bed, even finding a Beehive (The Golden Ones) in an old shack. It's a bitter lesson to learn that you can't fight the night without a proper hearth, but completing the shelter on the first try felt like Astrid’s Grace and ensured I followed the Rested (Shieldmaiden’s Rule) discipline.

**[Conrad's Chronicle]**
Upon the creation of the character Conrad and the generation of the world Terra Conrad, the player arrived in the Meadows via Valkyrie transport. The player immediately secured basic survival resources including leather scraps from a boar and meat from a Neck. After discovering the Vegvisir for Eikthyr, a stone axe was crafted to facilitate more efficient resource gathering. The player identified a ship-shaped rock formation and a beehive before crafting a hammer to establish a base of operations. This base included a campfire, a workbench, and a bed within a fully enclosed shelter. By sleeping in the bed, the player set their spawn point and successfully triggered the 'Rested' buff for the following day.
