# 🛡️ Gold Extraction Report: S01 E004

## 📝 Summary Table
In this episode, Grandpa Bud completes his first permanent base, survives a harrowing first raid by forest creatures, and begins the transition into leather armor and honey production.

## 🧠 Editor's Notes
The content is highly educational for new Valheim players. Grandpa Bud’s 'pro-tips'—specifically regarding floor leveling and chimney ventilation—are gold for Type A shorts. The first raid provides a necessary spike in tension to balance the serene building segments.

## 🪵 Ledger Entry (Narrative Summary)
> Day six and seven in Terra Conrad have been a testament to the Viking spirit of 'measure twice, cut once.' Grandpa Bud spent the morning hours wrestling with the architecture of his first real home, sharing the wisdom of leveling the earth to keep the dirt from peeking through the floorboards. Just as the roof was settled and the chimney began to draw air, the forest itself turned hostile. The first raid, led by the spirits of the wood, nearly saw the end of Conrad as health dipped to a precarious twelve points. But a Viking is not so easily felled. With the raid repelled, the focus shifted to the finer things: the sweet hum of happy bees and the crafting of leather armor. From salvaging old ruins for lumber to marking the map with the precision of a seasoned scout, Grandpa Bud is proving that while the meadows are serene, survival is a craft learned one log and one deer hide at a time.
