**[Grandpa's Legend]**
The Black Forest is a heavy place for an old soul, yup. I went looking for stone-marrow to strengthen my tools, but the forest-kin did not welcome me. It is a bitter lesson to feel the weight of a troll's tree-trunk, but a man must protect his home for the sake of the family. I spent some time with the soil and the swine to clear my head after the fighting. A sturdy buckler and a warm cape are the only things keeping the cold at bay now.

**[Conrad's Chronicle]**
In this chronicle, the journey for bronze turns into a desperate struggle for survival against the terrors of the Black Forest. After several deaths at the hands of a one-star troll and a forest bear, Conrad manages to turn the tide with a well-timed parry and a sharp blade. The conflict escalates when a troll attacks his forest outpost, necessitating a full rebuild of his kiln and forge. The session concludes with a return to the homestead to harvest carrots and upgrade his gear, leaving him better prepared with a max-quality bronze buckler and new troll skin attire.
