### 📖 THE LEDGER ENTRY (Strategic Analyst)

**The Strategy:**
You are teaching a "Safety First" philosophy. This is your brand's strength. While others rush to fight bosses, you show how to live long. 
*   **The Moat:** This is a foundational lesson. It transforms the game from a 'survival struggle' to a 'managed homestead.'
*   **The Copper Trench:** This is a high-level tactic. By trenching the ore, you create a 'safe room' in a dangerous biome. This is the 'Grandpa Wisdom' that viewers crave.
*   **The Raft:** You were honest about the raft being slow and frustrating. This builds trust. We will title that segment "The Slow Road to Progress."
